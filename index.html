Who Am I?

Like many I love to travel, but I've always wanted to go as a student or worker. Never as a tourist. Because of this I learned languages but I soon learned languages not because I was going to live in a certain country, but to find more and more obscure language styles. I developed a deep desire to learn how unique each language could be and how similar some concepts were across the globe. This lead to an interest in programming languages.

Everyone knows immersion is the best way to learn any language and am therefore beginning my coding journey as I began all my other language learning endeavers:

  1. Eager to learn
  2. Eager to use

As an avid language learner I want to collaborate on a variety of projects, with a variety of people. Don't hesitate to contact me no matter the human or programming language you know.
